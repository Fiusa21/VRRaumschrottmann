VR Raumschrottmann

Mission Briefing

Collect orbital debris, feed the core, and survive the rotating collectors.

Controls

  • Desktop: Click to lock cursor; WASD move, Shift boost.
  • Mouse: Hold left to pull debris, release to throw.
  • VR: Right controller trigger to pull and hold, thumb trigger to shoot, grip to throw; right stick to move.

Objective

  • Grab floating debris with your laser.
  • Toss debris into the orange rotating garbage collectors.

Tips

  • Throw with some space towards the containers
  • Stay mobile; the collectors orbit unpredictably.
  • Keep the laser active right until before pressing the release button for better aiming!
